About

Who we are

Coffee Support Society (CSS) is a small, not for profit organisation
that is interested in funding mental health initiatives and other charities such as NCBI and so forth.
Through buying our coffee grinds and beans, you are able to support local coffee farmers in delivering quality coffee to your homes,
free from chemicals and industrial grade pesticides that hamper the quality of your daily mug.

Stores

Currently we are a small business in Limerick, located in Arthur's Quay in the city center. However, we plan to expand to the rest of Ireland eventually through donations and helping other charity services.
At the moment we are located at 31 William Street Co. Limerick .

Services

Coffee:
We offer Coffee in a variety of forms, such as ground, beans and instant from various companies Eg., Starbucks, Costa, etc. The beans we sell are from ethically sourced coffee farms and plantations.

Counselling:
In addition to our Coffee, we offer free counselling and mental health support with professional
psychiatrists that we can redirect to you according to your personal budget.
